Who we are

Introducing the first-ever contract to offer a dividend tracker designed to track LP tokens, allowing LP holders to still recover their full $WCRO rewards while staking LP tokens in our Dapp and farming $ACCRO. Tokenomics

8% Buy Tax / 16% Sells Tax


2% of Buys/ 4% of sells – $WCRO Rewards
A total of 6% of transactions (2% of all buys & 4% of all sells) are redistributed to holders in $WCRO rewards. This reward system creates a passive income for investors holding $ACCRO. Holders also have the opportunity to create liquidity pair tokens to farm in return for $ACCRO in our DAPP and earn double rewards!

2% of Buys/ 4% of Sells – Buy Backs
A total of 6% of transactions (2% of all buys & 4% of all sells) go directly to our manual buy back wallet which will be displayed in our Dapp. This will allow us to make continual large purchases benefiting the chart and give us the liberty to use purchased tokens for additional pools, giveaways and burn activities.

1% of Buys/ 3% of Sells – Auto Liquidity Pool
A total of 4% of transactions (1% of all buys and 3% of all sells) will generate an auto liquidity to continuously pump the ground floor and create a healthy pool to counteract potential negative transactions.

1% of Buys/ 1% of Sells – Auto Burn
A total of 2% of transactions (1% of all buys and 1% of all sells) is automatically burned, creating a hyper-deflationary mechanic, perpetually lowering the circulating supply and stabilizing the floor, complementing our buy back and auto LP functions.

6% Marketing & Development
A total of 6% of transactions (2% of all buys and 4% of all sells) is transferred to the marketing wallet to support the continual growth and development of Accro Whales. The possibilities are endless as far as future listings, bridges, expansions, and exposure is concerned and the majority of this will be funded through the marketing wallet.

Buyback

An essential aspect of the Accro Whales contract is the buy back function. 2% of every buy and 4% of every sell goes directly to the buyback wallet, promoting price stability and further providing liquidity to farms, allocating tokens for the $ACCRO- $WCRO rewards and contributing to passive income opportunities for holders. We will be holding a series of buyback events at certain milestones within the community.

Rewards

Simply hold Accro Whale tokens ($ACCRO) to automatically receive Wrapped Cronos ($WCRO) rewards. 2% of every buy and 4% of every sell goes to the rewards wallet and distributed to holders proportionately based on the amount $ACCRO held. The more you hold, the more rewards you will receive.

Farming

As an extra incentive to our holders, we provide an LP farm. Anyone holding $ACCRO can take advantage of this opportunity to receive $ACCRO rewards for providing liquidity. A stable and progressive APY for LP holders creates an incentive for farming and the opportunity to take advantage of double rewards! Accro Whales is the first token to offer rewards for creating LP tokens and staking in addition to rewards for holding.
